User Data Policy

This page outlines how user information is handled within the GoNow experience and how account deletion requests can be submitted.

Delete Your Account and Data

If you want to delete your account and associated personal data, you can send a formal request to healthbridge.main@gmail.com.

  • Include your full name and the email address linked to your account.
  • State clearly that you want your account and personal data deleted.
  • Optionally include your registration date or any relevant account details to help us locate your request.

We will review your request and take appropriate steps to remove the information in accordance with applicable privacy practices.
